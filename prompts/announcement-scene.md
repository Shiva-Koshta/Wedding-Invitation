## Prompt
A romantic storybook scene of an Indian couple standing together beneath an ornate floral toran archway dripping with marigolds and gold drapery. Soft and dreamy, lots of gentle negative space around them so overlaid text stays readable. Candle-warm glow, celebratory yet serene.

Watercolor storybook illustration, Indian fairytale aesthetic. Palette: warm off-white background (#FAF6EF), antique gold (#C8A04A), champagne gold (#D8B56A), deep maroon accents (#7A1E1E), muted marigold-yellow florals (#E8B23A). Soft hand-painted edges, delicate gold-leaf linework, gentle aged-paper texture. No text, no lettering, no watermark.
