## Prompt
A single ornamental corner flourish for the top-left corner of a page: a graceful spray of painted marigolds, small buds, and curling gold filigree vines with a few soft leaves, radiating inward from the corner. Balanced and airy, decorative frame element. Transparent background.

Watercolor storybook illustration, Indian fairytale aesthetic. Palette: antique gold (#C8A04A), champagne gold (#D8B56A), deep maroon accents (#7A1E1E), muted marigold-yellow florals (#E8B23A). Soft hand-painted edges, delicate gold-leaf linework. No text, no lettering, no watermark. Transparent background, isolated element.
