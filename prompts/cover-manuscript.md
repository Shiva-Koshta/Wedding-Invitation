## Prompt
An ancient, aged manuscript page — warm ivory parchment with soft mottled texture and gently worn edges. A delicate ornamental gold border frames the page, with a few small hand-painted marigold sprigs and a single golden quill feather motif near the top center. Empty, calm negative space in the middle (text will be overlaid by the app). Ethereal, magical, candle-lit warmth.

Watercolor storybook illustration, Indian fairytale aesthetic. Palette: warm off-white background (#FAF6EF), antique gold (#C8A04A), champagne gold (#D8B56A), deep maroon accents (#7A1E1E), muted marigold-yellow florals (#E8B23A). Soft hand-painted edges, delicate gold-leaf linework, gentle aged-paper texture. No text, no lettering, no watermark.
